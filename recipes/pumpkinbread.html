Pumpkin Bread

This recipe is one of my favorites. Is so delicious that it is the most frequent bake my boyfriend and I do regarding the season just because it taste amazing and all the spices in it smell amazing and work great with each other.

Theres no need of a special ocasion to bake, but if you would like to surpice your famaily and friends this fall with a deliciouse and unique pumpkin Bread I totallhy recomend this quick recipe.

Ingredients

  • 250 g of Flour
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground cinamon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 170 g of soft butter (very important butter is not cold)
  • 400 g of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can or 425 g of pumpkin pure

Steps

  1. In a bowl add and mix all the dry ingredients. ( mix the fluor, salt, baking powder, baking soda, ground cloves, ground cinamon, ground nutmeg)
  2. In a separate bowl mix the soft butter with the sugar. make sure there are no butter lumps
  3. Once the sugar is incorporated with the butter, add the eggs and the pumpkin pure to the bowl
  4. Mix well
  5. Finally add the bowl with dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and make sure everything is well incorporated
  6. Preheat the oven at 325 F
  7. Once the oven is ready, pure the mix into a baking container and bake for 65-75 minutes
  8. Once baked let our deliciose Pumpkin bread cool for 10 min and enjoy!
